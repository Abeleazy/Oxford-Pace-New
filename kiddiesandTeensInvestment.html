Overview

Oxford Pace Kiddies and Teens Ltd is a subsidiary company of Oxford Pace Int'l Ltd (member company of Oxford International Group). Oxford Pace Kiddies and Teens Ltd is a company that is focused on children' future growth and their investments.
At Oxford Pace Kiddies and Teens Ltd, we have products for kids & Teens, in which you can investment in. We are giving every parent and guardian an avenue to insure the future of their kids through our products.

Real Estate Investment

We enhance our operations by relieving you of the worries associated with achieving your desired financial goals for your kids.

Land Investment

We help you acquire valuable lands and properties for your kids to help secure their future.

Our Mission

We partner with parent/guardian around the world in the fight to end unemployment among our youth. We build an healthy environment for our children, thriving and empowering them to create lasting change in their own lives and communities.

Our Vision

Building foundation for financial Independence towards achieving the highest possible standard for future CEOs across the globe.
